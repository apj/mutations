Allan Jones

Allan is a Bachelor of Science (Professional Software Development) at Swinburne University of Technology in Melbourne, Australia, completing his Honours year in 2010.

His major research interest is in software evolution, with a more focused interest in the evolution of features and source code vocabulary in software systems.

Bianca Rinaldi

Bianca is a 5th year student from Melbourne, Victoria, completing her Honours year in the Bachelor of Science (Professional Software Development) (Honours) course at Swinburne University of Technology.

Her major research interests are in software architecture and evolution, in particular if software developers make use of model separated code, and what the implications of its use have on a project and its lifetime.

Leonard Hoon

Leonard is a full-time postgraduate student from Singapore, attempting his final (and only) year of the Master of Information Technology course at Swinburne University of Technology in Melbourne, Australia, with the intended course completion date of end 2010.

While his current active research area is in the realm of Software Evolution, he also has interests in Software Engineering paradigms, and the advocacy of the User-Centred Design Paradigm.
